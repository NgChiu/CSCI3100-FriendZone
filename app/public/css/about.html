About Us

FriendZone is a website designed for people, mostly students, to expand their friend zones and social networks. In a society that people work or study all day and have nofree time, it is hard to find suitable time to meet friends. Our objective is to provide a platform which users can easily find people to spend good times together and meet new friends. This platform serves the purpose of linking people with same interests together. Users can host different activities, for example sports, study groups or simply to have a meal. We hope that via our platform, people can build a community where people share the same interest.

FAQ


What if I forget my password?

Please send a email to us and state the problem that you are facing. We would delete your previous account within 7 days and we will inform you when it is done. Then, you can register for a new account.

Why I can't join/create more than one event?

As we don't want user to be a "player". We have limited the users to join/create at most one event at the same time. Don't worry. If you want to join another event, you can simply quit the previous event and join the new one. Or you can wait until the previous event is over.

What if someone didn't show up during the event?

You can report the user in our "Report User" page. After the user is reported by someone, the RP mark of the user will be deducted by a certain value.

What is RP mark?

RP mark stands for reputation mark and will be deducted if that person violated some rules. RP mark will be shown along with the events to let others to consider whether joining that event or not.

Why can't I see any post when I am at the index page?

Every time you enter the index page, you have to press that orange "UPDATE EVENTS" button in order to show all posts. If there are still no posts shown after pressing that button, it is probably that there are no posts currently. You can consider create a new event on your own or wait for a new event created by others.
